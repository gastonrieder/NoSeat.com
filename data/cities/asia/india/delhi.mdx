## Getting to know the network
Delhi's public transport system is a vibrant mix of modern and traditional modes. 
The Delhi Metro is one of Asia's largest and most modern metro systems, complemented by an extensive bus network and the city's iconic auto-rickshaws.
Getting around on public transport is not just practical, it's the best way to experience India's bustling capital.

### Delhi Metro Smart Card
The **Metro Smart Card** is {frontmatter.metaTitle}'s reloadable card:
- Buy at any Metro station counter
- 10% discount on Metro fares
- Valid on Delhi Metro and Airport Express Line
- Minimum first load ₹100
- Refundable security deposit ₹50

### One-time Tokens
- Available at Metro stations
- Valid for single journeys only
- More expensive than Smart Card fares
- Must be returned at destination

### Cash payments
- Standard payment for buses
- Required for auto-rickshaws (ensure meter is running)
- Keep small denominations handy

## Airports

### Indira Gandhi International Airport (DEL)
- **Airport Express Metro**:
  - Direct connection to New Delhi Station
  - 25-minute journey
  - Runs from 4:45 AM to 11:30 PM
  - Accept Metro Smart Card or tokens

- **DTC Airport Bus**:
  - Multiple routes to different parts of Delhi
  - Air-conditioned buses
  - More economical than Metro Express
  - Cash payment to conductor

## Free and discounted travel
- Children below 3 feet height travel free on Metro
- Student concession passes available for buses
- Senior citizen discounts on Metro (with proper ID)
Visit the [Delhi Metro website](http://www.delhimetrorail.com/) for details.

## Making the most out of {frontmatter.metaTitle} using public transport!

 

### Best Metro routes for sightseeing

#### **Yellow Line**
- **Route**: Samaypur Badli ↔ HUDA City Centre
- **Highlights**:
  - Old Delhi (Chandni Chowk)
  - New Delhi Railway Station
  - Connaught Place
  - Central Secretariat

#### **Violet Line**
- **Route**: Kashmere Gate ↔ Raja Nahar Singh
- **Highlights**:
  - Humayun's Tomb
  - Lajpat Nagar Market
  - ITO
  - Mandi House cultural hub

#### **Blue Line**
- **Route**: Dwarka Sector 21 ↔ Noida City Centre
- **Highlights**:
  - Akshardham Temple
  - Indraprastha
  - Rajiv Chowk (Connaught Place)
  - Yamuna Bank

#### **Heritage Bus Route 405**
- **Route**: Mori Gate ↔ Red Fort
- **Highlights**:
  - Jama Masjid
  - Red Fort
  - Chandni Chowk markets
  - Old Delhi streets

#### **Route 100 Electric Bus**
- **Route**: Indraprastha ↔ Safdarjung
- **Highlights**:
  - India Gate
  - National Museum
  - Parliament House
  - Lodhi Garden
