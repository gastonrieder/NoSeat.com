## Ticket Types
- Single Ticket (Einzelfahrkarte): €3.50
- Day Pass (Tageskarte): €8.20
- 9am Day Pass (9-Uhr-Tageskarte): €6.90
- Group Day Ticket (Gruppentageskarte): €25.00
- Weekly Pass (Wochenkarte): €34.00

## Transport Network
- U-Bahn: 4 metro lines (U1-U4)
- S-Bahn: 6 suburban train lines
- A-Bahn: 3 regional train lines
- Ferry: 6 harbor ferry lines
- Bus: Extensive city coverage

## Zones
- Ring A: City center
- Ring B: Greater Hamburg
- Ring C: Metropolitan region
- Ring D: Extended metropolitan area

## Operating Hours
- U-Bahn & S-Bahn: 4:30 AM - 1:00 AM
- 24-hour service on weekends
- Night buses cover main routes overnight
- Harbor ferries run 5:00 AM - 11:30 PM

## Where to Buy
- HVV ticket machines
- HVV app
- Service centers
- Bus drivers (cash only)

## Tips
- Get HVV app for real-time updates
- Harbor ferries included in regular tickets
- Airport connection via S1
- Consider Hamburg CARD for tourists
