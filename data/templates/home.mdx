## NoSeat.co in a nutshell

Public transport is usually the most convenient and cheapest way to move around. 
It is also a great way of experiencing cities like a local. 

Google Maps and similar apps have made navigation easy... but paying for public transport is still confusing. 

**NoSeat.co** aims to fill that gap.
